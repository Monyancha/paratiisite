Features

A video player like any other. Except for what’s behind it.

  • Videos can be free or have different monetisation models. They all look similar, but some prompt users to validate micropayments
  • Adaptive bitrate streaming: at every point, users get the maximum quality for their device and connection. The file processing magic is done by a distributed network of transcoders
  • Users can earn tokens whenever they’re active. These are stored in an in-player wallet, and “excess” funds (think a content creator who’s getting a lot of audience) are transferred to a separate account
  • Casual users may watch for hours and still never notice this is not an average player. Those who sign up can claim tokens earned and use them to curate, acquire or promote videos
  • Multiple platforms supported. Sharing is incentivised if creators choose to divide a portion of their earnings with ‘promoters
  • Curation is left to the audience, who’s rewarded for spotting inappropriate content early on or pointing to copyright-infringing material

Components

What are we talking about?

THE PROTOCOL

Coordinates creators, publishers and users to maintain a decentralised exchange of videos and attention, that communicates receipts off-chain and settles compensations on Ethereum contracts. It also bundles access to all services needed to distribute media and metadata serverlessly and trustlessly

THE PTI TOKEN

Native staking token to the Paratii curated registry, it represents the right to belong to and judicate over a content list, and that of creating your own. The PTI can also be used as a means of payment for certain services, paid videos and content promotion. It is mintable: earn tokens by curating content, seeding videos while watching, or running a media node

THE PLAYER

An embeddable, modular and extensible player to customise as you wish. Apply it on your page, media channel or news outlet with a single line of code. Share it on social media when you spot one on Twitter, Medium or Telegram. With paratii-lib, developers can also build widgets and plugins to modify frontend and interact with specific modules of the protocol

Roadmap

A short recap and next milestones

Paratii has been under design and development for little over an year. Our vision is to provide an open market for audiovisual stories where any agent, independently of its size and bank account, has an equal chance to be heard or seen and profit off the network.

  1. NOV 2016 Team formation
  2. JUN 2017 Publish blueprint
  3. OCT 2017 Paratii foundation incorporated in Estonia
  4. DEZ 2017 Release Paratii player v0.0.1
  5. JAN 2018 v0.0.2: Paratii-lib and plugin-oriented player refactoring
  6. FEB 2018 Web-portal for uploading and browsing (whitelisted creators)
  7. MAR 2018 Release bluepaper + protocol completion
  8. APR-MAY 2018 Token distribution event (tokens functional on mainnet)
  9. Q2 2018 Open access to uploading + pilot publishers program
  10. Q3 2018 Migration to mainnet for remaining components
