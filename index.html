SEE THE WHITE PAPER

Power to Transform Individuals

Cryptocurrencies are digital tokens that can create an incentive layer for distributed networks. Paratii (or PTI, for short), is a token to be launched on the Ethereum network, the world’s second most capitalized blockchain and arguably the most powerful one. PTI’s main utility is to buy media, so every coin grants the right to a certain amount of impressions. And every impression holds the power to transform the individuals being impacted by the story being told to them.

Every Paratii Player is Yours

Paratii incorporates economic incentives into video consumption, allowing users to earn a share of the value they help create for the network. Access your wallet, transfer funds and check or use your reputation through an elegant embeddable video player that interacts with the Ethereum blockchain once you’ve become a node on the Paratii network.

FEATURES

Features

Collective Curation

Viewers of online video are already used to rating, flagging and recommending content. On Paratii, these activities will be rewarded.

Peer-to-peer streaming

Distributed file sharing allows for content delivery frameworks independently of private data centers, and at a fraction of the cost.

Automated Ad Selling

Media will be sold through real time bidding auctions, while traffic data will always be public and unforgeable.

Decentralised Governance

Paratii’s evolution is guided not by a private company, but by a community of people steered by a fluid reputation system.

SEEDS

The Seeds

Having no central authority requires decision power to be distributed and influence to be measured: precisely what a reputation system is good for. Paratii’s seeds are its first content producers, ambassadors and reputation holders. The values they represent set our north.

Amyr Klink

Royal Geographic Society member & Paratii first seed

Sailor, boatmaker, best-selling author and Royal Geographic Society member, Amyr was the first man ever to cross the Atlantic Ocean rowing (1984) and also to complete a non-stop solo circumnavigation aboard a sailboat in Antarctica. Being an economist, he sees in Paratii the potential to bring together nature, technology and social transformation in a way that’s never been done before.
