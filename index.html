READ OUR BLUEPRINT

Collective Curation

Viewers of online video are already used to rating, flagging and recommending content. On Paratii, these activities will be rewarded, and moderation incentivized rather than imposed.

Peer-to-peer streaming

Distributed file sharing allows for content delivery frameworks independent of private data centers, running at a fraction of the cost.

Automated Ad Selling

Media can be sold through future contracts or real time bidding auctions, while all traffic that's "paid for" gets publicly registered and unforgeable.

Fluid Governance

Paratii’s evolution is guided not by a private company, but by a non-profit, and community is steered by a dynamic reputation system.
